HOW TO MAKE INDIAN TEA?

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons tea leaves or tea powder
  • 1-inch (2.5cm) piece of fresh ginger, crushed
  • 4 green cardamoms, whole
  • Crystallized sugar
  • 1/4 cup warm low-fat milk

Steps

  1. Set a metallic saucepan on the stove top or hob.
  2. Add the piece of freshly crushed ginger and four green cardamoms.
  3. Add quarter cup of warmed low fat milk.
  4. Pour the solution from the saucepan to the teacup with the help of a tea strainer.
